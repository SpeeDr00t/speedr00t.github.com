To exploit this, wait for someone on your shared LAN to access a target host with Timbuktu. Sniff the traffic going from user to target host, tcp destination port 1417. The packets containing the characters typed will have (in the data segment) an initial hex sequence of "05 00 3E" each (followed by the uppercased character).
