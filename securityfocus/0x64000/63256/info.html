An attackers can exploit this issue by enticing an unsuspecting victim to follow a malicious URI.

The following example URI is available:

http://www.example.com/wp-content/plugins/dhtmlxspreadsheet/codebase/spreadsheet.php?page=&lt;script&gt;alert(1);&lt;/script&gt;
