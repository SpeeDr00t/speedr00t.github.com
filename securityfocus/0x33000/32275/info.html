The following example scripts are available:

Enable remote management on port 8080: /cgi-bin/system_setting.exe?remote_mgmt_enabled=1&amp;remote_mgmt_port=8080
Restore the router's default factory settings: /cgi-bin/restore.exe
Reboot the router: /cgi-bin/restart.exe
