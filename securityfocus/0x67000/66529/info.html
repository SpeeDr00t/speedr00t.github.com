Attackers can exploit the information disclosure issue through a browser. To exploit a cross-site scripting vulnerability, an attacker must entice an unsuspecting victim to follow a malicious URI.

The following example URIs are available:

Cross Site Scripting:
http://www.example.com/wp-content/plugins/js-multihotel/includes/timthumb.php?src=XSS

Path Disclosure:
http://www.example.com/wp-content/plugins/js-multihotel/includes/timthumb.php?src=Path Disclosure
